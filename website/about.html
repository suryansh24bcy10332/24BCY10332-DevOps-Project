Our Story

From Startup to Global Leader

Founded in 2010 in San Francisco by a group of passionate engineers and visionary entrepreneurs, ABC Technologies began as a boutique software consultancy with a singular focus: solving complex business problems through elegant technology.

What started as a 12-person team working out of a co-working space has grown into a global organization of 300+ professionals, serving clients across 35 countries. Our growth has been fueled not by aggressive sales tactics, but by the results we deliver — results that turn one-time clients into decade-long partners.

Today, ABC Technologies stands at the forefront of the digital transformation era, helping organizations harness the power of cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and next-generation cybersecurity to compete in an ever-evolving marketplace.

Our Mission

To empower businesses of every size with innovative, reliable, and secure technology solutions that drive measurable growth, operational excellence, and sustainable competitive advantage — while fostering a culture of continuous learning and ethical innovation.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted technology partner — a company where groundbreaking ideas become real-world solutions, where talent from every corner of the globe collaborates to shape a smarter, safer, and more connected digital future for generations to come.
